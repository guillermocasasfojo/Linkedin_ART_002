# Philips 12NC — Digits 1–2: Product Group (HIG)

The first two digits of a Philips 12NC identify the **Product Group**, in Philips terminology known as *Hoofd-Industrie Groep* (Main Industry Group) or **HIG** — the product axis of the Philips matrix organization.

> **Note on this reconstruction:** this table is a historical reconstruction based on publicly available sources, mainly historical references and vintage collections from the Philips ecosystem. Ranges 01–29 behave as *product categories* (materials and components) rather than true Industry Groups. Some entries reflect where a code range is observed today (e.g., in products of Philips spin-offs) rather than its original 1963 definition. Corrections and additions are welcome.
