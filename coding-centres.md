# Philips 12NC — Digits 3–4: Coding Centre

Digits 3 and 4 of a Philips 12NC identify the **Coding Centre** — the centre responsible for issuing the code, in many cases (but not always) equivalent to the product's country of origin or the country in which it was developed. This is the geographic axis of the Philips matrix organization.

The list started off as an alphabetical index of countries (with some anomalies — note, for example, Colombia at 05 and Nigeria at 17). Later coding centres were added in order of appearance rather than alphabetically.

> **Note on this reconstruction:** this table is a historical reconstruction based on publicly available sources, mainly historical references and vintage collections from the Philips ecosystem. Codes not listed are unknown so far. Entries marked *unconfirmed* are based on observations of individual products rather than documented definitions. Corrections and additions are welcome.
